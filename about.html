Our Mission

At Hasegawa Mwmoriel Public School, we strive to provide top-quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential. We focus on nurturing creativity, curiosity, and a love of learning.

Our History

Founded in 1985, our school has grown from a small institution into a center of excellence in education. We have a rich history of academic achievement and community service.

Our Values

We believe in fostering a learning environment that values integrity, diversity, and inclusivity. Our core values guide our actions and shape our future.
